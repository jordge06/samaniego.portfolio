My Skills

Debugging

I have experience working on a monolithic codebase that can easily reach thousands of lines of code per class. Through this experience, I've honed my debugging skills, enabling me to swiftly identify and resolve issues within the system. I am also familiar with the debugging tool IntelliJ Debugger.

Testing

I work for only two companies, both of which adhere to the concept called Test-Driven Development (TDD). I'm familiar with both unit testing and integration testing. I have written hundreds of tests using JUnit and perhaps a couple of tests using Postman.

Searching

I always strive for independence, though there might be instances where I seek help from other developers. I aim to work on my own problems as much as possible. With this mindset, I have developed the skill of searching for solutions to my own issues, utilizing helpful sites like Stack Overflow, GitHub, Reddit, and, more recently, AI like ChatGPT.

Adapting to changes

As mentioned previously, I work exclusively for two companies, both of which hired me as a Java Backend Engineer. However, there have been many instances where I've needed to learn new technologies to meet the needs of clients or the company. This includes becoming proficient in new programming languages, adopting new development technologies, and most recently, utilizing cloud platform tools such as AWS.

Communication

In my three years of experience, I've learned that, apart from coding skills, effective communication with coworkers, clients, or even bosses is a critical skill to acquire. Initially, I tended to conceal my mistakes when starting out as a developer. However, throughout my career, I've learned that not only does this not benefit me, but it's also unethical. Therefore, during our stand-ups, I prioritize transparency about my progress. While this sometimes portrays me unfavorably as a developer, I consistently strive for honesty and openness.

Guiding others

Teaching is a part of my experience that I really love. I find great satisfaction in helping others with their tasks, especially when I'm able to assist my coworkers. I've learned that offering help can significantly strengthen relationships with others.
